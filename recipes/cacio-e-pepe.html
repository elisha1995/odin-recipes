Cacio e pepe

Whip up a simple cacio e pepe for a speedy lunch

With four simple ingredients – spaghetti, pepper, parmesan and butter – this is a storecupboard favourite

Ingredients

  • 200g bucatini or spaghetti
  • 25g butter
  • 2 tsp whole black peppercorns, ground, or 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 50g pecorino or parmesan, finely grated

Steps to Follow

  1. Cook the pasta for 2 mins less than pack instructions state, in salted boiling water.
  2. Melt the butter in a medium frying pan over a low heat,.
  3. Then add the ground black pepper and toast for a few minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta, keeping 200ml of the pasta water.
  5. Tip the pasta and 100ml of the pasta water into the pan with the butter and pepper.
  6. Add a splash more pasta water if you need to, to loosen the sauce and coat the pasta.
  7. Serve immediately with a good grating of black pepper.
